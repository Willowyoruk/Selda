Our Story

At Selda Mediterranean Kitchen, we believe that every meal is an opportunity to share a story. Founded on the principle that the most memorable moments happen around a table, our kitchen is a tribute to the vibrant culinary heritage of Turkey. We combine time-honored techniques—like the mastery of our authentic charcoal grill—with the freshest seasonal ingredients to create a dining experience that feels both sophisticated and deeply familiar.

We are proud to be a part of the growing Selda family. Expanding from our roots in the Dallas area to the heart of San Antonio, our mission remains the same: to bring the authentic, soulful flavors of Mediterranean cuisine to our guests. While we have grown, our commitment to quality, Halal-certified ingredients, and genuine Turkish hospitality has never wavered.

Whether you are joining us for an intimate dinner or a celebration with friends, we invite you to experience the passion that defines every dish we serve. We are honored to be a part of the San Antonio culinary landscape and look forward to welcoming you to our table.
