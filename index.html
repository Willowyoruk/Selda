Welcome to Selda

Experience Mediterranean Excellence

Selda Mediterranean Kitchen brings the warmth and flavors of Turkish cuisine to Texas. Our carefully curated menu features authentic Mediterranean dishes prepared with the finest ingredients and traditional cooking methods.

From succulent kebabs to fresh mezze platters, every dish is a journey through the rich culinary heritage of Turkey and the Mediterranean region. Our upscale ambiance combined with exceptional service creates an unforgettable dining experience.

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or enjoying a casual evening, Selda welcomes you to our table.
