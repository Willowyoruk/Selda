About Selda

Selda Mediterranean Kitchen brings the rich flavors of Turkish & Mediterranean cuisine to Texas with a modern, elevated dining experience. Always fresh ingredients, authentic recipes, & warm hospitality.

Enjoy handcrafted kebabs, Turkish doner, lamb chops, grilled branzino, house-made hummus, falafel, and traditional pides baked fresh from the oven.
