About Selda

At Selda Mediterranean Kitchen, we bring the rich, timeless flavors of Turkey and the Mediterranean to the heart of San Antonio. Our kitchen is founded on a simple promise: elevated dining through authentic, time-honored recipes and the freshest seasonal ingredients.

From the heat of our traditional oven—where our signature pides are baked fresh to order—to our hand-marinated kebabs and grilled branzino, every dish is an invitation to celebrate. Whether you are joining us for our famous Mixed Grill, a vibrant Shepherd Salad, or a sweet finish of house-made Kunefe, you will find our table set with genuine, warm hospitality.
