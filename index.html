About Selda

Selda Mediterranean Kitchen brings the rich flavors of Turkish & Mediterranean cuisine to Texas with a modern, elevated dining experience. Always fresh ingredients, authentic recipes, & warm hospitality, Selda serves a wide variety of dishes including handcrafted kebabs, Turkish doner, lamb chops, grilled branzino, fresh seafood, house-made hummus, baba ganoush, falafel, & traditional pides baked fresh from the oven.

Guests enjoy signature dishes like Chicken Adana, Urfa Kebab, Ali Nazik, Iskender Doner, and our popular Mixed Grill, alongside vibrant salads including Shepherd Salad, Beet Salad, & Arugula Quinoa Salad. Complete your experience with classic Turkish desserts such as Baklava, Kunefe, and flan.

Cocktails, Beer & Hookah available.
